About ISKCON




Founded by A.C. Bhaktivedant Svami Srila Prabhupada in 1966 on the Instruction of His Guru - Srila Bhakti Siddhanta Sarasvati Thakur Prabhupada to "Spread the Conclusion of Vedic Scriptures - Krishna Consciousness in English Language." . Hence ISKCON was established by the following 7 Purposes




1975 - Srila Prabhupad gave HH Gopal Krishna Gosvami Maharaj (our beloved Shiksha Guru) instructions to preach in India




Srila Prabhupad Made HH Gopal Krishna Gosvami Maharaj GBC (Governing Body Commission) for India and gave him instructions to preach in India.Same Year Krishna Balraam Temple (ISKCON Vrindavan) was also establised .




1977,November 14 - Srila Prabhupada's Departure




After Establishing 108 ISKCON Temples all over the World (just what His astrological chart foretold) , Srila Prabhupada left this movement unto His Disciples. Due to Absence of Acharya , the Movement did Slackened but all the Sincere disciples followed the Instructions of Srila Prabhupada , following them thus , their Seperation was cured by folowing those Instructions




1980s - 2011




ISKCON has nearly 500 Centers all over the world - combining Temples , Eco-farms and Goshalas




2023




ISKCON is having 1000 centers all over the world (More than 830 Centers in the world and more than 120 Goshalas and Eco-farms) . Sadly , this is the same year when HH Gopal Krishna Gosvami Maharaj left us Physically .




2025 and onwards




We, at ISKCON Sonebhadra, are humbly trying to serve in Seperation Srila Prabhupada and HH Gopal Krishna Gosvami Maharaj by trying to follow their instructions




"To check the imbalance of values in life and achieve real unity and peace in the world."
